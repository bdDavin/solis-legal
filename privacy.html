Solis Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

Solis is developed and published by Ben Davin, Sweden. Contact: hello@trysolis.app.

2. What we collect

Nothing. Solis has no servers, accounts, analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, including with third-party AI systems.

3. Permissions and on-device data

With your permission via iOS prompts, Solis uses your camera (for the AR overlay; never recorded or transmitted) and your location while in use (to calculate the sun's path). Your preferences and recent searches are stored only on your device. Lawful basis under GDPR: performance of the service you requested (Art 6(1)(b)) and your iOS-level consent (Art 6(1)(a)).

4. Retention and deletion

All data remains on your device for the lifetime of the installation and is deleted when you uninstall Solis.

5. Apple frameworks

Map tiles, reverse-geocoding, ARKit, and Core Location queries are handled by Apple under Apple's own privacy terms. No personal data leaves your device through Solis.

6. Your rights

EU/EEA and UK users have the rights of information, access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and freedom from solely automated decisions. Because Solis stores nothing off-device, you can exercise erasure, restriction, and portability simply by deleting the app. For other requests, contact hello@trysolis.app.

7. Complaints

You may complain to Sweden's IMY, your local EU/EEA data protection authority, or the UK's ICO.

8. Children

Solis is suitable for all ages and collects no data from any user, including minors.
