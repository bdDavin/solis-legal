Solis Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Solis is published by Ben Davin. The app does not collect, track, sell, or transmit any personal data.

Data & Permissions

  • Device Storage: Your preferences and recent searches are saved strictly on your device. Deleting Solis deletes this data.
  • Camera & Location: Solis requests camera access for AR view and location access to compute the sun's path. This data is processed locally and is never sent to the developer.
  • Apple Frameworks: Maps and AR features rely on Apple infrastructure and are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Support & Children

Solis is safe for all ages and collects no data from minors. If you email hello@trysolis.app for support, your email address is exclusively used to reply to your inquiry.

Updates to this policy will be posted here with a revised date.
